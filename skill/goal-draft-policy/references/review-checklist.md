# Reviewed-draft checklist

Use this checklist only after the evidence pass and before presenting the draft.

- The request is actually for drafting or reviewing a Goal, not execution, ordinary planning, a one-off task, a `/goal` explanation, or continuation of an active Goal.
- One durable outcome is stated.
- Named evidence and verification surfaces exist, or their absence is explicit.
- Numeric targets come from cited pre-existing evidence; they are not invented from a measurable surface.
- Evaluation endings are unbiased and the contract cannot be rewritten after results are seen.
- The iteration loop selects bounded next actions from evidence.
- Success and blocked endings are task-specific; a single failed attempt is not a blocker.
- The response stops at an inactive, human-reviewable draft.
